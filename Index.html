Privacy Policy - Businessclub App

Privacy Policy (EN)

1. Data Controller

The Businessclub App is provided by Bizzapp B.V..

For privacy questions, contact: info@bizzapp.nl

2. Data We Collect

  • Profile data: name, email address, phone number (optional), profile photo, job title, company name, website, social media links, bio, interests.
  • Company data: company name, address, contact details.
  • User activity: profile visits, click behavior, events and news viewed.
  • Chats and messages: messages exchanged with other members.
  • Event data: event registrations and tickets.
  • Financial data: invoices and payments processed in our financial systems.

3. Purposes of Processing

  • Providing a member network and displaying profiles.
  • Facilitating chat and messaging between members.
  • Organizing and managing events.
  • Sending push notifications (e.g. new members, events, reminders).
  • Processing invoices and payments.
  • Statistical analysis to improve the App.

Important: Your data will not be used for commercial purposes and will never be sold or rented to third parties.

4. Retention Periods

  • Profiles, messages and chats: stored as long as your profile is active.
    • If you delete data or your profile, the data remains recoverable for up to 12 months.
    • After 12 months, data is permanently deleted.
  • User activity: stored for a maximum of 36 months.
  • Financial data: kept in accordance with legal retention periods (7 years).

5. Sharing of Data

  • Other members within your clubs.
  • Partner business clubs with which your club shares members.
  • Service providers for hosting, payments, and push notifications.
  • WordPress plugins displaying data from the App (no synchronization).

We will never sell or use your data for commercial purposes.

6. Data Subject Rights

  • Request access, correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

Requests via: info@bizzapp.nl

7. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including SSL encryption, access control, and activity logging.

8. Changes

The most recent version of this privacy policy is always available in the App.
